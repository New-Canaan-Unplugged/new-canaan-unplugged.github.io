New Canaan Unplugged

New Canaan Unplugged is a parent-run group dedicated to helping parents and kids in our community develop a more mindful relationship with technology. We operate completely independently from the New Canaan Public School System and the Town of New Canaan.

Here are some ways you can get involved with us:

  • Sign Up for our newsletter
  • Share this website with your New Canaan neighbors and friends
  • Join one of our upcoming events
  • Take our survey
  • Send us an email with how you'd like to help.
