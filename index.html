New Canaan Unplugged is a parent-led group dedicated to helping parents and kids in our community navigate a more mindful relationship with technology.

Here are some ways you can get involved with us:

  • Sign Up for our regular newsletter.
  • Join an upcoming event.
  • Take our survey or send us an email with how you'd like to help.

New Canaan Unplugged operates completely independently from the New Canaan Public School System and the Town of New Canaan.
